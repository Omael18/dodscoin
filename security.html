FAQ 🤔

Is the mint authority really revoked? ▼
Yes, verified on-chain. The mint authority was revoked at launch, meaning no additional tokens can ever be minted. The total supply of 1,000,000,000 DODSC is permanently fixed.
Can the developer rug pull? ▼
The LP is locked via StakePoint for 69 days, and the mint authority is revoked. This means the developer cannot create new tokens or withdraw liquidity during the lock period.
What happens after 69 days? ▼
The LP unlocks around August 22, 2026. After that, the community decides what happens next — whether to re-lock, extend, or take other governance actions.
Is the contract audited? ▼
DODSC is built on Solana's Token-2022 standard, which is a well-established and fully auditable standard. The contract is publicly verifiable on Solscan.
